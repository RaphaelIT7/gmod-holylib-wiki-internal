# Commits
Personally, I do like to force push quite often- this may affect the last commit or often up to the last 5 commits or so though I do try to not force push too many commits- so staying more than 5 commits behind should avoid conflicts.<br>
Because I like to force push, I will also take care of any issues caused for pull requests, so if you ever decide to contribute and your pull requests end up with conflicts due to me force pushing and changing existing commits, then you won't have to deal with that, leave it as is, and I'll take care of it once I consider the PR ready to be merged :)<br>

# Pull Requests
Pull requests are always welcome, if it's a new module, new function, a fix, a small change, as long as it's a valid change, I will consider it and most likely merge it.<br>
